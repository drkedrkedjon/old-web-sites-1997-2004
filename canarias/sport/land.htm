< SPORTS: EARTH>

The fact that we are located in Africa give us 365 days of perfect weather for any outside sport.

Tennis

As you can imagine there is hundreds of tennis playing field. Almost all better hotels have at least one if not more (ask your our operator). Few tennis clubs with many playing fields. Located all around island. Possibility to rent equipment

Golf

Up till now we had two playing fields. One in north of island close to Bandama volcano and other is Campo de Golf in Maspalomas on south of island. Now we have 4. In the nee of Faro is in process construction of new 5th golf course in Playa de Meloneras and that is also a brand new city built around. As for the plans, they are simple... Government is pushing to build up to 22 golf courses and to make this place as golf paradise. It is possible simply because you can enjoy perfect golfing in perfect weather conditions in middle of February. And you spent more money than average tourist. He, he, he...

Biking

Any, but especially Mountain Biking. Same as in other open sports, weather is playing main factor. Plus the unbelievable landscapes. RECOMMENDED especially south of island. I personally have passed some 8000KM in 2 years and it is beautiful to ride here. Once I have time I will write more information's. Not for the beginners and children at all. From intermediate till very difficult courses. 2-8 hours. Top of mountain is on 2000meters so if you go there you will have to sweat a lot...

Climbing or trekking

Could be beautiful experience if you are in walking king of sport. Organized with some clubs. They take you up with bus and you go down by walk. RECOMMENDED. You can go also from down to top but then you will be looked by those who go from up to down as crazy. (Or I did, since I was going with bike, not by foot, and they all sad to me that there is no more road up there... I did find one nasty track... and went on top of island)
