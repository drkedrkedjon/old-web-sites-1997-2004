The period of modern age is separated in three parts. First is development of canary society and economy after the colonization. Second is relations with Latin American continent and it colonization. Last is modern period in this century, with two wars, fraquizmo and democracy.

Economy after the colonization...

  One of first original "Canary" products, with success on world market was vine. It vas very successful product in Great Britain and her colonies in America. It has been famous by word "naturaleza" and it is even today that canary product are described as healthy and natural. After crisis with vine export, manu start to develop other cultures on their lands. Cereals, potato & tomato. Fishing start to develop in 18 century especially in Gran Canaria and become one of main food resources.

Canary society has split in 4 groups:

1. Aristocracy, with best land and political influence,
2. Commercial bourgeoisie, represented mostly by British,
3. Bourgeoisie agrar, those who work as layers or in other professions or live from receiving of same kind of rent (Land or housing...)
4. Last is bigest group of people who rent land and work in fields. They have lowest soc. status.
