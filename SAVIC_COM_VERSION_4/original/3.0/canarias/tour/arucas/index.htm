Arucas
 


    Arucas is well known by his vegetation and also by his Cathedral dedicated to St. John the Baptist. You should also visit old center made of blue stone. Visit Gourié Park with distillery of rum and museum. Many banana plantation are here.

    Restaurants:
-Mesón La Montaña
-Rincon Canario
-La Barca
-El Fielato

    Shopping

-Fruits and excellent rum
-You can by interesting"piedras de canteria"

    Places to visit

-Jardin de la Marquesa, botanic garden
-Monte Doramas, natural park
