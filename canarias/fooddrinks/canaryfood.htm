< CANARIAN FOOD SPECIALTIES >

Pure nature can be slogan... and welcome to rare place in world where you can eat good natural food.

First fish.

As you can imagine we eat tons of fish. If you are interested in fish you may go in Arguineguin, but not necessarily. It is small fishing village with few very interesting restaurants with fresh fish. Usually they serve "plato de dia", selection of different fishes cached same day and I recommend you to order the same, if not you can get frozen fish. Also if you are with more people ask for some nice big fish prepared in oven and completely covered with salt. It is fantastic. Ask for "pescado a la sal". RECOMMENDED. Note that if you order some fish you will always be served with frozen fish. Like tun fish. There is no fresh tun fish on this planet. If you want to try good fish ask for "cherna" or "corvina" or "cipirones" or "puntitos de calamar" or finally ask for "San Kocho"

Tapas,

If you are not for fish ask for: "Pimientos de padron" + "Papas arugadas con mojo picon" + "plato combinado de quesos Canarios artesanos, semiduros y tiernos" + "plato de Jamon serano" + "Oliven" & red vine "vino tinto El Grifo de Lanzarote" It will be expensive if you order in tourist area but perfect taste is irresistible. RECOMMENDED as it is typical local food and you will like it... especially effects of "pimiento de padron"

Salads,

ask for one made with famous canarian tomato & fresh local stuff.

Fruits,

if you are lucky you will find fruits "de pais" This "de pais" minds "made in Canarias" and do not forget to ask for "Platano de Canarias" and "Naranjas de pais"

After the food ask for "Gofio" o "crema Canaria"
