Other sports
 



    Well, you can, a part of biking, windsurfing, fishing and golfing, also walk... This is not joke. Many tourist agencies organize walking excursion up in mountains. They take you with bus and later return in town. Very interesting!
    Also all kind of water activities that you can imagine: sailing, surfing, waterscuters, boats, parachuting and so on... All that in Puerto Rico.
    Tennis... and other ground sports... everywhere...

    And of course you can play football, but you do that at home, so why should not?
