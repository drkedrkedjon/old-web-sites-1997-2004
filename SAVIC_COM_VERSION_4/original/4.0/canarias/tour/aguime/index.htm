Agúimes

 

 

    The town was formerly a bishop fief, and is one of oldest parish on island. Visit beautiful Cathedral and historic building, former Episcopal palace. Today municipality is target of large investors and big industry. Sandy beaches and strong wind are ideal for windsurfing.
