All about Gran Canaria, Spanish island of Canary archipelago, that is situated some 200 kilometers from Africa coast. Weather is maybe most important thing in Gran Canaria. Famous Dunas de Maspalomas are here. Sandy beaches of Playa del Ingles and San Agustin also. Capital city is Las Palmas.
