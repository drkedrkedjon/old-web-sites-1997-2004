Pepe, Artist from Gran Canaria. He makes his pictures with spray paint, performing his work directly in front of people on street of Playa del Ingles, in Gran Canaria, Spanish island in Atlantic ocean. His motives are surrealistic combination between futuristic visions of future like space and past elements like pyramids. His use spray paint, and hi limit use of brush on rare occasions...
