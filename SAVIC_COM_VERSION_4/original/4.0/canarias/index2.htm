   This Spanish island is situated some 200 kilometers from African coast. Excellent weather and low prices make this island one of best tourist destinations in Europe...
   Besides millions of tourists that came, here lives some 650.000 habitants. They have their own rich culture and tradition. Found out who are those warm friendly people that live under this "sunny island"...
