Teror

    City well known by festival in September that celebrate "Virgen del Pino"(Queen of the sea). Interesting wealthy and spiritual area. Try local mineral water.

    Restaurants:

-Miraflor
-Balcon de la Zamora
-Bar Diego
-El Comedor
-Tarsadea

Interesting to try are sweets that can be found in Plaza del Pino, specially marzipan.
