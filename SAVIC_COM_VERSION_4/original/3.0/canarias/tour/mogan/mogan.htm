Mogán
 


    Mogan is small fishing village which maintained also his production of vegetable & tropical fruit. Interesting is that in Mogán, water came from underground, which is rare in island. Village is some 10 km away from Puerto de Mogán, Beautiful marina village with menu restaurants and sport activates. Try to take a ride in submarine.
