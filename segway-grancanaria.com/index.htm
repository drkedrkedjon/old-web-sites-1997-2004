Now Available In Gran Canaria!
Segway "Tours & Walkabouts"



Introducing the great new way to enjoy your holiday on Gran Canaria.

See the grin burst on your face as you slalom along the pavement on an electro-powered Segway i2! In less than five minutes you can be trained up and ready to use one of our Segways, safely and comfortably.

Expect to smile your head off straight away! Well, at least that's most people's response to this spectacular gliding experience that can now be yours for as little as 15€.

And don't worry if you are not super fit. Segway is so easy to use that the only two prerequisites are that:

     1) you can climb the stairs by yourself
     2) you are over 12 years of age and weigh between 45kg and 115kg.

There are two different packages on offer.

The basic one we call Holiday World "Walkabouts". It's an afternoon indoor gliding session happening within the Holiday World Leisure Centre and lasting 20-25 minutes. It's ideal for those people who wish to master 'walking' before they start 'running'.

For those more adventurous of you we also offer morning "Maspalomas Segway Tours" of the Maspalomas area (more of a 'running' thing than a 'walking' thing). These are excursions approximately 12 kilometers long taking you to the beach and beyond and guaranteed to give you one stunning experience to remember.

Whether you opt for a "Walkabout"or a "Tour"there will be an instructor to guide you along the way.

We are limited to 6 people per group (+guide) so it is advisable that you book your place in a group online and in advance. (This is particularly the case during the busy summer months when we experience higher than normal level of bookings).

Personalized tour routes are available for groups of 4+. Contact us also if you are interested in purchasing a Segway and you want to test ride it. Prices and details are available on request.

Note: On all our Tours and Walkabouts brand new Segway i2 and x2 models are used exclusively.
