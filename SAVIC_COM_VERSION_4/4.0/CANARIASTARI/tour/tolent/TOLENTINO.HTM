San Nicolás
deTolentino

    To visit San Nicolás you must go by impressive road from Agaete. Don't miss Cortijo de Guayedra, Thenesor Semidan and village of Risco. Also Tirma rock and Güi-Güi with many zoological and botanical specimens. From the road you can see Tenerife. Very spectacular view. The town doesnt relay on tourism. Agriculture is main ocupation. Try to visit local market.
