Weather Specialities...

   There is few canarian specialities when We talk about clime. First is wind that comes from influence of anticyclone in Azores. It is always present, in same direction and in constant power, depending on season, that make this island "the place" for windsurfing. Of course wind is more important for local people, because it brings heavy humidity from Atlantic ocean.
   Humidity, pushed by wind in upper levels of island mountains, fall on land in form of "horizontal rain". This is second speciality of canarias. Horizontal rain is mostly present in autumn and winter and mostly in north of island. Sometimes when anticyclone goes more on south it bring cold weather and normally raining.
   When anticyclone goes more on north it brings "Shiroco", nasty wind from Sahara with sand and no humidity at all. It brings temperature in upper 40. This is next speciality.
   And maybe the last speciality is diversity of clime in one island. You can drive around island in one day and change all weathers situation in few hours. You will catch warm rain in north, fog and snow in mountains and sunny beaches in south. You newer know...
