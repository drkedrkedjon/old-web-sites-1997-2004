Telde
 


    Telde is second most important city on island. Interesting to visit is Gothic-Mudejar church of San Juan Bautista (Moslem art). Also museum of Fernando de León y Castillo worth your attention. Visit incomparable Caldera de los Marteles that stands at 1.500 meters. Airport is located in this Municipality.

    Restaurants:

-La Posada
-La Pardilla
-Bahia Mar
-Taberna Canaria
-Pablo Silva

    For visit:

-Mountain Quatro Puertas
-Lomo Magullo, natural area
-Las Marteles, natural area
