<BIG BEACHES>

They are all sandy, long, and crowded with people. But if you like to be on the beach where everybody is, with all possible services & sport activities, than this is the place to be. Basically there are three big beaches: Playa de Las Canteras, Playa del Ingles & Playa de Maspalomas. I will also include a few smaller: Playa de San Agustin, Playa de Puerto Rico, ...

Playa de Las Canteras

Playa de Las Canteras is situated in the center of Las Palmas. It is well known for beautiful yellow color of sand. Mostly visited by residents of Las Palmas. Nice possibility to meet them. Of course, a great number of tourists is also present. It is naturally protected by reef, so the beach is safe from big waves (or big fishes, if you ask me) and is suitable for small children. The beach is surrounded by commercial area with many restaurants, cafe's, shops etc. Starting from there is city center, so if you feel bored and want to take a walk, do it on the streets of Las Palmas.

Playa del Ingles.

One of the most known beaches in Europe, or in Africa if you like. Famous Dunas de Maspalomas mix with ocean there. It is overcrowded in most parts of the year & is three kilometers long. There are some big waves on one part, depending of wind direction. In the near by is big commercial center with many restaurants, bars and shops. (Mc Donald also, if you are looking for plastic food) Basically, you can found what ever you need on beach. Sport activities like jet ski, sailing, water skiing and etc. are also available. Part of the beach is reserved as nudists area.

Playa de Maspalomas.

Where Playa del Ingles ends, begins Playa de Maspalomas. The beach is two kilometers long, with orientation on south so in the Summer time is mostly free of big waves. At the opposite end is small shopping center with restaurants and shops. Natural area named "Oasis de Maspalomas" with bird nesting area and "Faro de Maspalomas", light tower are there.

Playa de San Agustin

The smallest and less crowded in this group. Beach is only few hundred meters long, but is sandy and well protected against waves. Close to beach is Shopping center. Besides tourists You will share beach with residents in weekends & in summer. Crowded sometimes, but nice and quiet beach. Recommended. Suitable for small children.

Playa de Puerto Rico

Situated in small tourist city some 20 km. From Maspalomas. Mostly British tourists area. It is small, sandy & overcrowded. Very well protected, with big yacht marina and shopping center. This area is center for water sport activities. If You like sport, here is place to be.

Playa de Amadores

Brand new artificial beach with huge shopping center. The beautiful yellow sand is brought from India (They say that but I do not know is it correct or not) Great beach even it is not natural one. RECOMMENDED few hundred meters from Puerto Rico.
