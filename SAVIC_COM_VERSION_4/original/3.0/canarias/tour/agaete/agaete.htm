Agaete
 

 
   Agaete is small fishing village. It is situated on side of mountain massive named Tamadaba, area of pine forest. In port you can see famous monolith called "Dedo de Dios".
    There is great number of restaurants with excellent fresh fish. Many Canary people are coming here in weekends to eat fresh fish. Also ferry to Tenerife goes from here.

 
 Restaurants:
-Casa Nando
-Mi Hermano
-La Granja
-El Dedo de Dios
-Las Nasas
-El Puerto de Laguete

 

   Shopping

-There is nice handwork shop in harbor and in Hotel Guayarmina.
-In Valle de Agaete is possible to shop excellent coffee, made here. It has full flavor. Recommended.


    Also visit near bu "Necropolis de Maipes", historic cemetery and "Huerto de las Flores", small botanic garden with exotic flowers.
