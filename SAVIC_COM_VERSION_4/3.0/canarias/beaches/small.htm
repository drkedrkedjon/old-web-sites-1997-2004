Small beaches
 



    It is almost quite useless to write names of all small beaches, since there is hundreds, and they are all around islands. So I wont. I will list only few of them.
    But there is few areas and advice's to follow. Here they are:

     North of islands between Las Palmas and Arinaga have many small beaches. They are specially good for surfing(not windsurfing), so if you are the one, there is place for you. Also in this area there is no tourists so menu days you will found yourself alone on beach...

    Area between Maspalomas and Puerto de Mogan is full of accessible small beaches but many of them are crowded because it is only 100% tourist area. And menu of them is for nudists or gay people, so make your choice as you wish. You can reach them by bus or by car.

    And basically where ever you go, you will found beach...

    Las Palmas

    1. Cicer, small beach next to Las Canteras. Menu young people make surfing.
    2. El Confital, excellent. Situated on Isleta.
    3. La Laja, the big beach.

    Arucas

    1. Las Salinas, place where they make desalinization of water. Nice but on heavy ocean is dangerous.
    2. El Puertillo, few apartments and restaurants. Lot of people in summer.
    3. Los Charcones, possibility for underwater fishing and surf, but many rocks make surf dangerous.
    4. San Andres, surfing place...

    Guía

    1. San Felipe, excellent small natural beach. Very relaxing ambient. One restaurant.

    Gáldar

    1. La Guancha, near by Aborigen cemetery. If you like fishing, here is one of best places
    2. Las Palomas, few nudists to found.
    3. Sardina, nice beach with few apartments and restaurants.
    4. Matorel, on weekends menu people make camping.
    5. Bajas de Matorel, dangerous access but excellence for fishing and diving.

    Agaete

    1. Las Nieves, many visitors on weekends. Excellent fish in many of small restaurants.
    2. La Palma, you have to walk for while till you reach beach, but it is worth.

    Artenara

    1. El Risco, popular camping area

    San Nicolas

    1. Güi-güí, black beach. It is natural paradise and mostly empty. Access with boat or you have to walk some three, four hour. Recommended if you have time to visit.
    2. Tasarte, nice fish restaurant.

    Mogan

    1. Veneguera, beautiful beach with possibility for fishing.
    2. Puerto de Mogan, next to marina.
    3. Taurito, nice beach.
    4. Medio almud, fishing area.
    5. Cura, big but menu tourists.
    6. Tayro, Balito, Anfi, Patavalaca, Arguineguin... all nice beaches but in middle of tourist area and mostly overcrowded.

    San Bartolomé de Tirajana

    1. Chica, nice small beach.
    2. Los Bigotes, nice for camping.
    3. Corral Blanco, interesting for diving.
    4. Arena, zone nudist.
    5. Meloneras. Big with many people, small restaurant.
    6. Aguila, windsurfing...
    7. Tarajalillo, windsurfing...
    8. Cardon, big beach with not so menu people.
    9. Caleta, next to marina. Fish restaurant
  10. Barranco de Tirajana, very futuristic scenography.
  11. Pozo Izquierdo, maybe best beach on world for windsurfing. Came here to watch them...
  12. Bahia formas, windsurfing...
  13. Barquillos, windsurfing...

    Agüimes

    1. Zoco Negro, quite and nice for swimming.
    2. Cabrón, nice fauna and ecosystem to se.
    3. Vargas, windsurfing...

    Telde

    1. Ojos de Garza, excellent fish restaurant.
    2. Agua Dulce, quite nice beach
    3. Las Salinetas, excellent.
    4. Melenara, menu visitants but nice.

 

Have a nice day.
