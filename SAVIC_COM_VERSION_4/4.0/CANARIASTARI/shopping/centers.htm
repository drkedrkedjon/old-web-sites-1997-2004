Shopping centers

    I will seriously recommend to you that all of your shopping do in shopping centers. Any street vendors are unsafe. Here in Gran Canaria everything is located in shopping centers.

    Las Palmas:

    There is top class house named "El Corte Ingles". Do not miss opportunity to visit it. Unfortinatlu class goes with price. It is most expenses but probably best here.
    "Las Arenas" is second important center. Menu small boutiques and shops. Huge megastore "Continente"also there.
    "La Ballena", relatively new center. Nice prices.

    Playa del Ingles:

    "Cita" Mostly German place. Gold, watches, electronic.
    "Jumbo" Similar to Cita in day time. Over night gay scene is here
    "Faro2" Good place for cloth shopping, little bit expensive
    "Kasbah & Metro" Some shopping but you will probably spend night there. It is night life center here.
     There is couple more centers like one in San Agustin but they are not so important.

    "Puerto Rico"
    It have only one Shopping center and there is available everything. Mostly English place.
