Holiday World "Walkabouts"

Available every evening:

19:00 - 23:00 (Sunday to Thursday)
19:00 - 24:00 (Friday & Saturday)

If you are thinking of visiting Holiday World for an evening of fun on a Roller Coaster & Sky Drop, plus to have a few games of bowling and a good meal then, why not also check out our *"Walkabouts"*? They are our Segway gliding sessions lasting 20-25 minutes and taking place inside the Leisure Center Holiday World.

Each session begins with a crash course that teaches you how to use the machine. That's followed by some personal warm-up practice. Once you feel you can do it we'll take you and the rest of your group for a real glide around the Ferris Park where you will Segway in front of the crowd! To make the ride more exciting we'll go to the upper tier as well and back down again. You will love it!

To summarize, "Walkabout" is built around 5-10 minutes of training & practice, followed by a 10-15 minutes supervised ride which means no riding on your own is involved. All in all, that's some 20-25 minutes of great fun!

Typically, there would be a "Walkabout" session every hour, although that can vary according to customer demand. If you don't find us at the starting point when you get there, the chances are we are in action and you just need to bear with us a few minutes until we are back. We promise you, it's quite worth waiting for!

Price: 15€ per person. Sorry, advanced bookings are not available on "Walkabouts". Discounts only available for groups of 4+. Personal sessions also available: ask at our shop for details.

Important Rules:

1. Weight limit for Segway use is 45 - 115 kg.
2. Minimum age is 12. Children aged 12 to 16 must be accompanied by a parent.
3. Children aged 16 to 18 can join the ride only if their parent agrees with it.
4. We reserve the right to deny service to any person who, in our view, is under the influence of alcohol or drugs and/or is deemed by the guide not to comply with our safety rules. So please, do Walkabouts before you do other things...

During winter months working time will be adjusted according to the park opening times which is usually one hour earlier (opening & closure) then normal.
