Tejeda
 


    Probably most impressive landscapes on island. Two colossal rocks: The Nublo and Bentayga (ex. volcanos) dominate area. Visit National Parador with restaurants and artesanial products. Area well known by Almonds. Try local marzipan and "bienmesables", Canarian dessert.

    Restaurants:

-Parador de Tejeda
-El Refugio
-Cueva de la Tea
-Sombra del Nublo
-Timagada

    Shopping:

-Sweets in "Dulceria Nublo" and "El Almendro en Flor"

    For visit:

-Cruce de Tejeda
