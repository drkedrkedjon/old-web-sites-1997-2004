<ABORIGINES, THE ANCIENT HABITANTS, PART 1>

Time...

Man first arrived on the Archipelago somewhere in the year 2000 BC The first were a group of Cro Magnons from Europe that settled in North Africa. Other races such as eastern and Mediterranean peoples probably originated as result of invasions from Africa and mixed with people already living here. Before the arrival of the Castilians in l478 many visited and trade with locals. First were Phoenicians traders and colonists. Around the year 500 BC, the Carthagenians traded with their inhabitants.

Origin...

As always many theories and ideas about origin of Aborigines. Some search for mythology persons and civilizations, some in biblical research, some in antic nations and people. As a meter of fact, menu believe that lost continent named Atlantida were here on this islands. Well, believe is believe but we will follow archeological researching. And archeology say that Aborigines came from north of African continent. Unfortunately it is still unclear why they came here. Was it because of desertification of Sahara and less humidity in Maghreb, or it is because some political, (Romans), reasons. It is probably that depending of groups and time, some came by they wishes, some for political, some for economic reasons.

People...

On Canarias the population is formed over two groups of people. One lived in La Gomera, Tenerife and interior the Gran Canaria. Second group were present in Gran Canaria and El Hierro. Even today the political & economical structure is separated between Gran Canaria and Tenerife. Population was small on Lanzarote, Fuerteventura, El Hiero, La Gomera & La Palma (1000-4000), and Tenerife & Gran Canaria had somewhere about 30.000 habitants pro islands.
