    FOLKLORE

    There is interesting folklore music on Gran Canaria. Music is always accompanied with dance. Characteristic instrument are:
    Timples - Like small guitar with 4 or 5 wires. Produce nice sweat sound.
   Also they use other wire instruments. Some groups use flute and percussion's.

    Principal types of music that is found on island are:
    1. Folías, in ritmo 4/4
    2. Isas, in ritmo 3/4, allegro
    3. Malagueñas, in ritmo 3/4, triste, weary hard to sing
    4. Baile de Gorgojo, historically danced in places occults.
    5. Baile del pámpano roto. Interesting incorrect dance.

    The most popular folklore group is "Los Gofiones".

    LITERATURE

    Best known writers from island are:

    1. Bartolomé Cairasco de Figueroa, (Las Palmas 1539 - 1610). Autor of monumental poetic work named "Templo Militante".
    2. Benito Pérez Galdós, ( Las Palmas 1842 - Madrid 1920). Never fully accepted on his land. Five times nominated for Nobel price. Most important work is "Episodios Nacionales".
    3. Nicolás Estévanez y Murphy, (Las Palmas 1838 - Paris 1914). Minister of war in First Republic. He newer return in Spain. Poet and great humanist.
    4. Tomás Morales Castellano, (Moya 1884 - Las Palmas 1921). Modernist, known mostly on islands. Famous works include: La poesia del mar, Las rosas de Hércules...
    5. Francisco Guerra Navarro. Humorist, creator of "Pepe Monagas" one fiction person which is very popular on islands.

    SCULPTURE

    Most famous sculptors are:

    1. José Luján Péres, (Guía 158 - 1610). The most famous religious sculptor ever. His work can be found on almost all churches on island.
    2. Martín Chirino, (Las Palmas 1925). Dedicated his life to island. Now director of Centro Atlantico de Arte Moderno in Las Palmas.
