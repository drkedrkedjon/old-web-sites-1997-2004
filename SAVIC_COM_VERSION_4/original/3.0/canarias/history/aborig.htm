The Aborigines...
 


Time...

  It has been calculated that man first arrived on the Archipelago some where in the year 2000 BC. The first humans to arrive on the island were a group of Cro Magnons from Europe that settled in North Africa. Other races such as eastern and Mediterranean peoples probably originated as result of invasions from Africa and mixed with people already living here. Before the arrival of the Castilians in l478 many viseded and trade with locals. First were Phoenicians traders and colonists. Around the year 500 BC, the Carthagenians heard of the existence of the Canaries and traded with their inhabitants.


Origin...

  As always many theories and ideas about origin of Aborigines. Some search for mythology persons and civilizations, some in biblical research, some in antic nations and people. As a meter of fact, manu believe that lost continent named Atlantida were here on this islands. Well, believe is believe but We will follow archeological researching.
  And archeology say that Aborigines came from north of African continent. Unfortunately it is still unclear why they came here. Was it because of desertification of Sahara and less humidity in Magreb, or it is because some political, (Romans), reasons. It is probably that depending of groups and time, some came by they wishes, some for political, some for economic reasons.


People...

  On Canarias the population is formed over two groups of people. One lived in La Gomera, Tenerife and interior the Gran Canaria. Second group were present in Gran Canaria and El Hierro. Even today the political & economical structure is separated between Gran Canaria and Tenerife. Population was small on Lanzarote, Fuerteventura, El Hiero, La Gomera & La Palma (1000-4000), and Tenerife & Gran Canaria had somewhere about 30.000 habitants pro islands.


Housing...

  The geological structure of islands determined way of living and housing. excluding Fuerteventura and Lanzarote, where they use volcano pipes, Aborigines lived in natural caves. Also with natural caves they use artificial. We can found presents of artificial caves mostly in Gran Canaria. They are oval caves with more than one rooms, and with magazines & etc. Some form of windows are also in use. Many of caves have geometrical paintings on walls. On Gran Canaria were also some housing on superficies. Oval houses with one or more rooms made from rocks without any cementing.


Politics...

  On Gran Canaria civil political institutions was Guanarteme, elected from nobles close to dynasty. Religion were in hands of Faycan, member of Guanarteme. Besides those were two more. One civil made from: Guanarteme, Faycan and different nobles, who had function of communicating with king about questions regrading government and justice. Second was military named Sabor, and is possible that functionality was only in times of crisis.


Economy...

  On all islands, except Gran Canaria, basis de economy was pastoral. cabra, sheep and pig were present. Agriculture is on secondary position. On opposite, Gran Canaria and north de Tenerife were agricultural with high technical development. Also fruits and coastal fishing bring goods to people. In the field of production is present ceramic, but mostly made by family for they necessities, wood industry is present very little, production of tolls is made on basis of rocks, because there is no metal on islands. At end, of course, many products made from fibre vegetal and leather.


Religion...

  There is three levels in hierarchy of Canary religion. first is God Superior, second is spiritus, and last is demons and enemy of man. On all islands were one God Superior, except El Hiero where we found two: Masculine and feminine called Eraorazan & Moneiba. On other God is cold differently: Acoran, Achaman, Orahan, Abora... God were collective and for society only. He is called only in great needs, like long dry periods. There were many rituals to "call rain". Second level was for different clans and is represented with idols and figures. Demon is called Gabiot or Guayota. Deaths are mostly funeral in caves. Of course it depend on social structure also. There was also mummification and on Gran Canaria we found tombs in area with superficial housing.


Society...

  Social structure of Canarias is tribe. There is great different in same islands. On Lanzarote and El Hierro is only one tribe. On other, tribe area is called "guanartermato, bandos, menceyato or reinos" depending on islands. System was decentralized except Gran Canaria, with more complex system. The society was not equally for everybody. On Grand Canaria existed nobles which married between them for reasons of keeping status. They were separated in two parts "caballeros" with pure blood and "hidalgos" second class. The most of population belong to class of "plebeyos or villanos". It is interested that different classes have different cloth, hair or bear style. Also justice was different on different classes.
