Artenara
 


    At 1200 meters, Artenara is highest municipality in Gran Canaria. Town is situated on edges of cliffs from both sides. There is great number of caves built in past, and many of them are still inhabited. Areas named Acusa seca & Acusa Verde is worth of your attention. I seriously recommend visit here. The landscapes are extraordinary.

    Restaurants:
-Rest. La Esquina
-Rest. Mesón la Silla
-Bar La Cuevita

    Shopping
-There is one shop in center. Ask for "aceite de almendra", exotic handmade oil.

    For visit:
-Tamadaba, national park
-Cuenca de Tejeda with interesting geology
