Gran Canaria

    Spanish island of Gran Canaria is one of seven that make Canarian archipelago. They are situated some 200 km from west African coast.
    Excellent weather makes them one of most popular tourist destinations in Europe. Gran Canaria is volcanic island. Endless landscapes of wild nature, with deep canyons and high peaks are mixed with long sandy beaches

     If you are going to visit island, do not stay only in tourist areas. Take a excursion to interior of island. You will be amazed with this continent in small. From high rocks of west coast, to agricultural north. From green areas on top of mountain to desert south with Dunes de Maspalomas.

    You newer know what you can found here. Try local food. It is excellent. Or maybe you are interested in night life. There is hundreds of places to go. Like sports? Windsurfing on famous Pozo Izquerdo beach perhaps. Or golf in winter, or mountain biking...

    Long time ago this was not easy place to live. Today is, perhaps. You must see landscapes to understand people that live here. You must be here to found that paradise...
