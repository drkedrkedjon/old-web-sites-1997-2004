They are sandy, they are long, and they are crowded. But if You like to be on beaches where everybody can meet everybody, that is the places. Basically there are three big ones:
      Playa de Las Canteras
      Playa del Ingles
      Playa de Maspalomas.
I will include and two smaller:
      Playa de San Agustin
      Playa de Puerto Rico.
Those two beaches are not so big, but they are main & equally crowded.

Beaches,
the big ones...

Playa de Las Canteras.

     Playa de Las Canteras is located in the center of Capital city Las Palmas. It is well known for yellow color of sand. Mostly is visited by local Spanish people who live in Las Palmas. That bring possibility to meet local people. Of course great number of tourists is also present. It is naturally protected by reef, so swimming is no problem, there is no big waves. Or big fishes, if You ask. The beach is surrounded by walking street area with a lot of restaurants, cafes, terraces, etc. Starting from next street is city center, so if You like to walk, walk on streets of Las Palmas.

Playa del Ingles.

    One of most known beaches in Europe. Famous Dynes de Maspalomas enter in ocean on that beach. It is over crowded in most part of the year. She is three kilometers long. On same parts are big waves, depending of wind. On the beach is big commercial center with many restaurants, bars and shops. Basically You can found what ewer you want there. Sport activities like jet ski, sailing, water skiing and etc. are available.

Playa de Maspalomas.

    In continuity of Playa del Ingles is Playa de Maspalomas. The beach is two kilometers long, with orientation on south so in Sommer time is free of big waves. On the end is small shopping center with manu restaurants and beach shops. There is also located famous Oasis de Maspalomas with bird nesting area and "Faro de Maspalomas", light tower. Parts of beach is also nudists area.

Playa de San Agustin

    The smallest and less crowded beach in group of big ones. She is only few hundred meters long but is sandy and well protected against waves. In the nee of beach is Shopping center. Besides tourists You will share beach with residents here in weekends. Crowded, but quiet beach. Recommended.

Playa de Puerto rico

    Located in small touristic city some 20km. from Maspalomas. Mostly British tourists. It is small, sandy & overcrowded. Weary well protected, with yacht marina and shopping center. This beach is center for water sport activities. If You like sport, here is place to be.
