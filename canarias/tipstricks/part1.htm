Sun Protection

Please, once you arrive and before you burn your skin, use a skin protection products. Start with at least factor 15, or more even better (30 is not to much). To expose white fresh unprotected skin on direct sunlight could be very dangerous. Children under age of 3 must stay in shadow. Me to! It is not bla, bla, bla, from doctors. Do protect yourself and than you can relax on the beach. Bu the way, a side of danger, fresh burned red skin looks less sexy. Could be your skin...

Fast Food

Please be aware that here, as anywhere else in the world, there are some dirty places. Look for details. More clients, better. Check the food, do not use ketchup if it is already on table and not brought from refrigerator with hamburger. Specially look for small burger places. Otherwise bon appetite...

Food in villages

If you rent-a-car and go in mountains and villages please look for small restaurants in villages near by the roads. They are safe, and with fantastic experience as you meet with local villagers and eating same food as they, with prices that are unbeatable.

If you ever need a Dentist

If you need dentist I can seriously recommend to you Dental Clinic BLANCODENT in Las Palmas. They speak English, German & Spanish. The Clinic is situated in downtown business center of Las Palmas. RECOMMENDED To visit their web site please go to

Post office

There is only one big post office in Playa de Ingles - Maspalomas area. If you need anything more than to send post card you have to go there. Also note that there is private providers in tourist areas that is not on so high reputation... maybe your post card will not arrive on time...

Surfing

Please if you want to go to surf o north of island (not windsurfing) please be aware and do inform yourself by local surfers before you go to water. The beach for surfing located on north of island is VERY DANGEROUS Still I believe that some of you are stupid enough to go by themselves. Good luck

Rent-a-bike

If you are really in "biking" you better bring your own mountain bike. It can be done for free if you say to your tourist agents. It is not that here we do not have good bikes for rent, we have them, but they are usually already rented in advance and since the off roads are very hard and remote, you do not want to have some tech. problem with bike in middle of nowhere. Also can be problems with size. If it is only to cruse around Playa del Ingles than it doesn't matter

Negotiating the price

Please note that if you shop in shops owned by Hindu & Arab people (you will) you are expected to bargain the price. You can even bark on them. If you do not you are running in danger to be described as stupid. Also on the other places (some owned with same Hindu and Arabs) do not do that barking... as you can be glued as primitive...
