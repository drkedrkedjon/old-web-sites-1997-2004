The bungalow has newer been rented to any tour operator before and is used by owner since it has been built. Only recently it is available for rent directly from owner and only via this web site. For more details please use meny bar on left side. For photos & images use meny on top bar.

Resort

Las Vegas Golf resort is build some 12 years ago and is famous for nice bungalows surrounded by beautiful gardens with palm trees and flowers. Last year the pool have been rebuild and nice restaurant renovated. There is supermarket inside of resort with regular prices. It is located some 5 minutes of walk from golf course "Maspalomas Golf" and share entrance with best tennis center in south.
There is no permanent residents an area, neither night life, so it is very quiet and enjoyable for rest. Of course in 15 minutes of easy walk you will get to excellent shopping center FARO 2 that have to offer big supermarket, good shopping, good restaurants and lot off fun in bars in evening. The bus stop is in 5 minutes of walk from resort and from there you can reach any destination in island. There is rent a car office inside of resort if you find yourself in need to go in some more exploring. The beach with sand dunes is some 30 minutes of walk in shadow or you can use bus (every 20 min) or taxi that will not cost more than 2€

Bungalow

You will find bungalow in perfect conditions. It is fully equipped place ready for living as owner do spent few month a year in bungalow. Inside you will find 2 new refrigerators (one for drinks & other for food with freezer), then washing machine, cafe machine, small oven, small music center, TV set with international language stations. Also the kitchen is completed with everything that you will ewer need to prepare a food.
There are two beds in one room and if there is more persons there are two comfortable beds that can be arranged for sleep in day room. It is best suitable for family with few kids, for one couple or for few friends. Also it is all flat around without steps so it is also suitable for persons that may need that.
There is no room service available. The few sets of sheets, blankets and towels with cleaning before and after your holiday is included. Also there is washing service in resort if you do not want to use wash machine. For longer stays (1 month or more) cleaning can be arranged with additional price.
