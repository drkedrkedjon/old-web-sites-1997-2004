Gáldar
 


    The town is island first capital. It has name "Royal city" because the place is home of Guanartemes & Royals in the accent times of Aborigines. Don't miss to visit "Cueva pintada", artistically decorated cave. "Audiencia caves" where Councils were held. Agujero necropolis & Túmulo de la Guancha. Also very pretty church. If you have time try to visit volcano crater los Pinos de Gáldar, 1700 meters in altitude. Fantastic panoramic view
