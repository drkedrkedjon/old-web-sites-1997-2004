Santa María
de Guía
 


   The town is situated some 37 km from Las Palmas. It is well known as birthplace of many artists. You should try local cheese "queso de flor". There is Parish church, with nice baroque facade. In the nee of town is Valerón caves, homes of vestal virgins Harimaguadas.     Lot of craftsmanship. Don't miss possibility to by knife made by locals. Very nice product

-Bar Los Pescaditos
-Bar Los Quesos

    Shopping:

    For visit:

-"Cenobio de Valeron", more than 300 caves and "El Gallego", also many caves
