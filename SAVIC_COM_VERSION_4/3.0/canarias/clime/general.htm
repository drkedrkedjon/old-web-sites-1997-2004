Weather, general
 


    The temperatures on Grand Canaria is always pleasant. Medium temperature is somewhere around 18º grad Celsius. It does not varieties many over day, or as a meter of fact, over all year. The main influence on weather have anticyclone situated in zone of Azores. It brings pleasant and warm clime & warm waters. Local people maybe will not agree with description of clime as forever spring, but for somebody from European continent, Christmas on sunny beaches means forever spring. Basically on Gran Canaria almost never raining. The water comes from meteorological phenomena called "Horizontal rain".
     Another Canary specialties is wind. So important for "horizontal rain", and very cooling for people. It is always present and bring best windsurfers on the world here on beach Pozo izquierdo. To not bee all best there is two things that are not nice. Schiroco, very nasty hot wind from Sahara filled with sand. Second is when anticyclone is to south than the wind coming from cold areas, and it is raining vertically.
