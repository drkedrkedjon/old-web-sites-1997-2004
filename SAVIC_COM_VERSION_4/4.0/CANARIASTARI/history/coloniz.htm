Colonization
of Canarias

Knowledge about Canarias...

  First knowledge about Canarias came in accent age. Even Romans speak about archipelago. We can read about it in works of Homero, Plinio. etc. On the opposite side Europeans until 14th. century seems have no knowledge about islands. They came under spot after roads to orient has closed for reasons of Muslim occupation.


Conquest...

  The process begins in 1402, with Lanzarote and ended in 1496, with Tenerife. There were two levels. One called "señorial", and second "realenga". First is made by Juan de Bethencourt, with beck up in private & business money. He take Lanzarote in 1402, Fuerteventura in 1405, and also La Gomera and El Hierro. Second is with support of officials & monarch and they take Gran Canaria 1478-1482, Tenerife 1484-1496 & La Palma.


Status of Aborigines...

  Conquest brought many changes to Aborigines. From ecological, to social. Europeos brought new plants, illnesses and social system. Aborigines mostly stay with old activities, but some, who collaborate with europeos, receive land and better status. Those who did not collaborate were sold as slaves or sent on other islands. Anyway process is not same for all classes and members of Guanarteme receive titulo of Don.


Social system...

  Colonization of archipelago destroy old system in all levels. Also those who accept new system also hade to except new language, economy, politic, religion... etc. On social organization there were three levels: dominate, depended and slaves. In the first class were europeos represented with: Castellanos, Andaluces, Gallegos and Portuguese, and also aborigines who collaborate with them. They have land and influence on political and economic system. Second class were mostly represented by aborigines. They have small propitiates in land or work in handwork field. Slaves are minority from Africa and some of aborigines. They mostly work in sugar lands.
