If you are purchasing golden Rolex from African vendors on street for the price of 30€ try to understand that it is fake Rolex, same as golden chains that some of them sell, and if it is OK for you than note: it is up on your responsibility and is illegal. On the other hand the silver from Indonesia is looks original...

If you still can find that it doesn't taste the same then, maybe, you can start to think about stopping your addiction on tobacco. It will probably kill you one of these days.

Real Estate

Everybody can purchase real estate in Gran Canaria. It doesn't meter are you from EU or from outside and can spent as much time there as you want. Anyway please try to understand that if you spend more that 6 month in Gran Canaria you must have legal resident status and than you have to pay tax in Spain regardless of your country of arrival. If you are from outside of EU it can be long process since different laws apply for non EU residents. Check with Spanish embassy in your country for details.

Tax

The difference between tax is that on Gran Canaria consumers do not pay what they pay to Spanish government in the rest of Spain (some 15-18%). Here we and you have to pay only 4.5%. That is why some thinks are cheaper here then there. Like fragrances, new cars and so on. On the other side some products like simple newspaper is more expensive because of extra transport. So it could be paradise or hell...

Driving

First, in Spain the car that is inside of oval have the advance. Do not stop inside, please. It is OK to brake the golden rule of right side, left side advance but do not brake inside the oval! Also if the view is nice and you slow down to look, do not forget that somebody can be behind you, and could not be interested in that gorgeous peace of rock.
