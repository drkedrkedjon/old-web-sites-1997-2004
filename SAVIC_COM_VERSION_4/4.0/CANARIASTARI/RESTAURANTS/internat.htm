International cuisine

    Talking about international cuisine in Gran Canaria is not difficult. With so many tourists to serve, many interesting peoples who make living making food, from all around world found this island as home and open restaurants. You will found fare east restaurants as China, Thai, Japan... Many people from Cuba, so here is chance for little bit of Carib cuisine. Do not miss Argentinean beef. I think it's best on world. Indian restaurants maybe. If you like curry here they are. Also few African and Arab restaurants. Of course all European cuisine is present: Italian pasta and German sausages. Scandinavian and Swiss... etc.
    I must mention that all those restaurants are located in urban areas. You wont found them outside big cities and tourist areas.
