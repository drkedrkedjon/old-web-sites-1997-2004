INTERIOR

In interior of bungalow are one day room, one bedroom, bathroom and kitchen. There is also small azotea inside that is for free use. There are two beds in bedroom and day room can be arranged with two comfortable provisional beds. It is suitable for one couple or family with children or group of friends. Kitchen is fully equipped with electric equipment and day room also have radio & TV with international stations.
