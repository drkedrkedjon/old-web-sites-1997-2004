Food specialties
 



   On almost every corner of island can be founded many restaurants that serve Canaru specialties. I seriously recommend to spend Avery possible moment in those restaurants. Canary specialties are not most popular on world but here is three reasons to try it:

    1. It taste excellent and original
    2. It's cheaper than international
    3. Is made from plants and meet and fish that grows or live here where there is no industry and pollution. It is clean fresh healthy food and it taste GOOD!
