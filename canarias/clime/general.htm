<CLIME, IN GENERAL>

The Clime on Grand Canaria is always pleasant, no it is BEST clime on normal part of world. Temperature is somewhere around:

12º-17º night, 16º-24º day, in winter period (newer cold)
20º-24º night, 26º-32º day, in summer time (newer hot)
(This is my personal experience and not official statistic)

Temperature does not change during the day, or as a meter of fact, over all year. The main influence on weather have anticyclone situated in zone of Azores. It brings pleasant and warm clime & waters. Many people like to say that description of clime as forever spring is perfect, You can enjoy Christmas on sunny beaches.

Basically on Gran Canaria almost never raining. Last real rainy day I saw yesterday... No, I am joking, there is few weeks of unstable weather in December & January and it could rain for few days. The water for drinking and for plants comes from meteorological magic called "Horizontal rain".

Another Canary specialties is wind. So important for making of "horizontal rain", and very cooling for people. It is always present and bring best windsurfers on the world here on beach Pozo Izquierdo.

Not to not bee all the best, there is one thing that is not nice. Calima, very nasty hot wind from Sahara filled with sand. It arrive here from time to time. Will not disturb your holiday because it is hot and sunny period. But you have to go to cool yourself in ocean every 5 min...
